About Me

I'm Dayani De Silva, a third-year Information Technology undergraduate at SLIIT with a strong interest in Full-Stack Development, Quality Assurance Engineering, and UI/UX Design. I enjoy developing modern web applications using the MERN stack and Java Spring Boot, applying concepts such as MVC architecture, RESTful APIs, JWT-based authentication and authorization, and automated testing with Playwright. I am passionate about building secure, scalable, and user-friendly software solutions while continuously expanding my technical knowledge.

Before beginning my university studies, I completed a 6-month internship at the Bank of Ceylon (BOC) under the Job Skills Development Program, where I developed strong communication, teamwork, and problem-solving skills in a professional environment. I enjoy learning new technologies, collaborating with others, and transforming ideas into practical solutions through clean, maintainable code and thoughtful user experiences. Currently, I am seeking opportunities to gain industry experience and contribute to meaningful software projects.
